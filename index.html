Courses

Shall We Talk

4-semester online course that includes speaking and vocabulary practice. Best suited for students with prior English language knowledge.

SBS

SBS (Side by Side) classes are one of the longest running in our school. They are taught by both Chinese and foreign teachers back to back. Best suited for primary school students with prior English knowledge.

WTE

WTE (Welcome to English) classes are taught using books and materials that have been developed specifically for Chinese students. With that in mind, they give great context for English language learners in China. The classes are designed for students with low to no prior English language knowledge.
